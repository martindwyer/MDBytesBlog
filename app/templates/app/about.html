About
Martin Dwyer
Founding Contributor

Martin Dwyer is owner and operator of md Bytes , a web development concern out of Cedar Rapids, Iowa. In addition to numerous professional certifications in web development, Martin has also achieved an M.S. in Information Technology with emphasis in Software Engineering. Many of the articles written here were first conceived as part of his journey toward the M.S. degree.

Martin's career spans decades of leadership in top companies which include Merrill Lynch, US Bank, Wells Fargo and Regions Bank.   But perhaps his biggest decision was prompted by a small business client he assisted while a consultant at GoDaddy.  This customer hit on a recurring theme, i.e. website developers are either impossible to find or too expensive to hire.  Since that time, his ventures have taken him from being a licensed reseller of GoDaddy web hosting to a reliable provider of small business websites and applications.

Martin's favorite professional endeavors include professional development through open sharing with others. This blog was conceived as an important means to that end.
